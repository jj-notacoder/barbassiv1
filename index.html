About Us

Founded in 2019 and rooted in Abu Dhabi's Port Zayed, Barbassi is more than a focaccia kitchen and café, it's a gathering place built on family values of hospitality, creativity, and community.

Our menu centres on a tight selection of standout sandwiches, refreshing drinks you won't find anywhere else in town, and the kind of food that sparks a conversation rather than stealing one.

Because that's what we believe — food lives in the background, and the real magic is the people it brings together.
